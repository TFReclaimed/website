# Glossary of Terms and Abilities

<ins>Action</ins>: A move, attack, or trait activation. Units may normally take
1 Action per turn.

<ins>Active</ins>: Active Units may move, attack or utilize their activated
trait.

<ins>Anti-Personnel Weapon</ins>: +1 Attack versus **Soft Units**.

<ins>Armor</ins>: Combat damage is first dealt against Armor. When there is no
Armor, the remainder is dealt to Health. At the end of each player's turn, Armor
resets. Armor is like a form of shield.

<ins>Armor Piercing</ins>: Damage that ignores Armor.

<ins>Assault (Pilot)</ins>: Activates on first attack. Does not work while
embarked in a Titan.

<ins>Assault (Titan)</ins>: Activates on first attack.

<ins>Atlas Titaneer</ins>: +1 Attack and +1 Health to your Atlas Titans.

<ins>Auto-Titan</ins>: A Titan deployed and not joined with a Pilot.

<ins>Barrage</ins>: **Deal Damage** to all Units in a targeted region. This
damage ignores **Armor**.

<ins>Battlefield</ins>: Playable area that includes the **Commanders**, both
**Perimeters**, and the **Frontline**.

<ins>Blocked</ins>: Attack or **Counter Attack** combat damage is nullified.

<ins>Bounty</ins>: Target a Unit and mark it. When the Unit is destroyed,
trigger a beneficial effect.

<ins>Bunker</ins>: When a Homesteader is Destroyed, the next Commander card
drawn costs 1 less Command. No more than -1 Command can be in effect.

<ins>Burn Card</ins>: A card that provides a temporary beneficial effect and is
discarded once it has been played.

<ins>Challenge</ins>: Battle the enemy Unit until one or both Units are
**Destroyed** then move into the **Frontline** afterwards if still alive.

<ins>Cloak</ins>: Avoid the first attack each turn while Cloak is active.
**Counter Attacks** are still effective against Cloak. Does not work while
embarked in a Titan.

<ins>Combine</ins>: 2 Pilots combine, taking the best Attack, **Armor** (if any)
and Health stats and then adding +1 Attack and Health. The new Unit counts as a
single Pilot until destroyed.

<ins>Command (Points)</ins>: Command is used to deploy cards from your hand. You
may not have more than 10 Command.

<ins>Commander</ins>: A card that represents the players in matches. They can be
used in Commander Decks and Frontier Missions.

<ins>Counter Attack</ins>: Units that are attacked with base combat
automatically counter-attack the Unit that attacked them.

<ins>Data Knife</ins>: Target Auto-Titan, Spectre, or **Installation** is
**Stunned** for 2 turns.

<ins>Deadeye Weapon</ins>: +2 Attack versus enemy **Commander**.

<ins>Deal Damage</ins>: Target immediately takes **Armor Piercing** damage
without the opportunity to **Counter Attack**.

<ins>Defender</ins>: Cannot move into the **Frontline** region.

<ins>Deploy</ins>: Activates on deployment onto the **Battlefield**.

<ins>Destabilization</ins>: When this Unit destroys an **Installation**, **Deal
2 Damage** to all Units in enemy **Perimeter**.

<ins>Destructive Weapon</ins>: +2 Attack versus **Installations**.

<ins>Destroy</ins>: Target is immediately removed from play without the
opportunity to **Counter Attack**.

<ins>Deter</ins>: Target Unit's traits are disabled until the end of the next
turn.

<ins>Discard</ins>: Opponent discards one or more random cards from hand into
the discard pile.

<ins>Dodge</ins>: Avoid the incoming attack, canceling the combat.

<ins>ECM Module</ins>: The first time this Unit enters the **Frontline**, cannot
be the target of Burn Cards or effects until your next turn.

<ins>Eject</ins>: The pilot escapes the piloted Titan that is destroyed and
moves into an open available space in the same region.

<ins>Electric Smoke</ins>: **Cloak** and **Deal 2 Damage** to any **Soft Units**
which attack this Unit until your next turn.

<ins>Exploit</ins>: **Deal 1 Damage** to target enemy Unit.

<ins>Frag Grenade</ins>: **Deal 2 Damage** to all enemies in target enemy
region.

<ins>Frontline</ins>: The 3 locations in the center of the **Battlefield** where
Units may attack their opponent's **Commander**. Only pilots and Titans may move
into this region.

<ins>Hack</ins>: Effect a **Hard Unit** with a Hack. _(Hack includes: Data
Knife, Puppetmaster, Stun Hack, System Breach, Data Breach, Wifi Virus, Jammer
Station and Packet Sniffer)_

<ins>Hard Unit</ins>: Mechanized or Structural Target (Titan, Support
Installation, Support Turret, Spectre or Spyglass).

<ins>Heal</ins>: All wounded **Soft Units** are healed for indicated amount.
Heal also removes **Stun**.

<ins>Heavy Weapon</ins>: +1 Attack versus **Hard Units**.

<ins>Hidden</ins>: This Unit appears different that it actually is.

<ins>Inactive</ins>: Newly deployed units are Inactive and may not move, attack
or utilize their activated trait. Passive traits are immediately **Active** on
all Units.

<ins>Infiltrate</ins>: Opponent **Discards 1** random card from hand.

<ins>Installation</ins>: A type of Support Unit that represents a structure on
the **Perimeter** of the **Battlefield**.

<ins>Intercept</ins>: Force all enemies to attack this Unit first.

<ins>Last Laugh</ins>: Activates on death.

<ins>Leadership</ins>: All of your other Units gain +1 Health.

<ins>Medic</ins>: On deploy and at the beginning of every turn, all wounded
**Soft Units** are **Healed** for indicated amount. Heal also removes **Stun**.

<ins>Most Wanted</ins>: Place a Most Wanted marker on enemy Unit. All attacks
against that Unit gain +1 Attack.

<ins>Nuclear Ejection</ins>: Damage is doubled against Units in the
**Frontline** if this is used in the **Frontline**.

<ins>One Shot</ins>: Trait which can be activated only once. Activate by
pressing on the Unit for 3 seconds then drag to a target.

<ins>Ogre Titaneer</ins>: +2 Attack to your Ogre Titans.

<ins>Parkour (Kit)</ins>: This Unit may move without taking an **Action** and
ignores **Intercept** when attacking. Does not work while embarked in a Titan.

<ins>Passive</ins>: A trait that is always active.

<ins>Perimeter</ins>: The large area on the **Battlefield** for each player next
to the central **Frontline** region where most Units are deployed.

<ins>Pilot Bonus</ins>: Titans add the Attack value of a Pilot when joined. Most
Pilot passive abilities also work while piloting.

<ins>Protect</ins>: Your pilots with **Intercept** gain **+3 Armor** to until
your next turn.

<ins>Puppet Hack</ins>: Gain control of targeted **Hard Unit** and give it
**Rush** until end of turn. If it is piloted, the pilot is ejected.

<ins>Quickshot</ins>: Unit may attack, but not move or activate a trait
immediately after deploy.

<ins>Repair</ins>: All damaged **Hard Units** are healed for indicated amount.
Repair also removes **Stun**.

<ins>Repair Titan</ins>: Cannot move into the **Frontline** or be piloted. On
deploy and at the beginning of every turn, friendly **Hard Units** are
**Repaired**, removing **Stun**.

<ins>Repeating</ins>: Activates on deploy and at the beginning of your turn.

<ins>Repel</ins>: Force Units to move out of the **Frontline** back to their
**Perimeter**.

<ins>Resilience</ins>: Cannot be targeted by enemy Traits, Secrets, or Burn
Cards.

<ins>Rodeo</ins>: Gains +2 Attack, **Armor Piercing**, and will not be **Counter
Attacked** this turn when attacking a Titan.

<ins>Rush</ins>: Unit is immediately fully **Active** after deploy.

<ins>Sabotage</ins>: **Destroys** target enemy **Installation**.

<ins>Secret</ins>: Secrets are played on a specific target, trigger an effect
given a specific condition, and are played hidden from view of the opponent.

<ins>Self-Destruct</ins>: **Destroy** a **Unit** to gain a triggered action.

<ins>Soft Unit</ins>: Human Target (Pilot, Support, or Commander).

<ins>Sniper</ins>: Attack that cannot be **Counter Attacked**. The Attack is
increased by the listed value. Does not work while embarked in a Titan.

<ins>Spot</ins>: Target a Unit and apply an effect onto it.

<ins>Stryder Titaneer</ins>: +2 Health to all friendly Stryder Titans.

<ins>Stun</ins>: Target is unable to perform any **Action**. This includes
movement, traits, attacks, and **Counter Attacks**. Lasts 2 turns.

<ins>Support Titan</ins>: On deploy and at the beginning of every one of your
turns, **Repair** friendly **Hard Units** for **2**. Cannot move into the
**Frontline** or be piloted.

<ins>Suppress</ins>: Target enemy Unit has their Attack reduced by the listed
value until your next turn.

<ins>Targeting Module</ins>: This Unit has +2 Attack.

<ins>Titan Shield</ins>: +1 Armor at the end of any turn health is damaged.

<ins>Titan Speed</ins>: Titan may take an extra **Action** this turn, as long as
it is not the same **Action** twice.

<ins>Tutorial Protocol</ins>: Tutorial Combat Drones have reduced health and
reduce their opponent's starting hand size.

<ins>Units</ins>: All Units on the **Battlefield**, including the **Commander**.

<ins>Warpfall (Transmitter)</ins>: This Unit may deploy on an unoccupied
position of the **Frontline**.

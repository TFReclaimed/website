# Collectables

<ins>Credits</ins>: Earned from Missions, Head-to-Head matches, and sometimes
booster packs.

<ins>Intel</ins>: Gained from the Frontier War and rarely from booster packs.
Used to access special missions.

<ins>Reputation</ins>: A mechanic currently disabled that may become useful with
further development.

<ins>Supply</ins>: Earned over time (up to 1000), from special missions, and
sometimes from booster packs. Used to start Frontier Missions.

<ins>Tokens</ins>: Earned from completing Frontier Missions. Primary method of
unlocking booster packs. Can be used to revive injured units.

<ins>XP</ins>: Earned from Frontier Missions, Head-to-Head matches, Booster
Packs, and hourly Gifts from Guild members. Used to level up Pilots and Titans.
_(Go to the Hangar, tap the desired Pilot or Titan, then tap 'Apply XP'.)_

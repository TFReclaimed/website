Check out all the new stuff in Frontline 26.4.3!
You will need to update your game to continue playing.
Simply do the installation process again like you did the first time, no need to uninstall the game first.

{/* truncate */}

- Added auto surrender after 1.5 minutes of inactivity in a match
- Made tokens purchaseable with credits in the store
    - Exchange rate is 1000 credits for 10 tokens
- Tweaked booster pack odds, unique cards will be preferred over duplicates, but duplicates can still be obtained
- Fixed a bug where you would get kicked out of a match if you had an action queued after the enemy commander's death
- Added the online counter to the chat too
- Changed update popup text to be less confusing
- Fixed long guild descriptions being cut off
- Improved leaderboards scrolling with inertia
- Likely fixed a bug causing you to be unable to send chat messages
- Fixed the french language causing mission percentages to be displayed as 0%
- Increased the matchmaking timeout from 20 seconds to 2 minutes

## Update on Assault

Updating to 2.1.4 is still ongoing, so far we're loading into the menus again most stuff there works as it did before!

Unfortunately Particle City decided that part of the tutorial now includes 3 online AI training matches,
implementing these will take a lot of work but in the end we will probably be better off with them since then
we will be able to always ensure that players have something to battle against.
